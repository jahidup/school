About Us

Building Character,
Not Just Careers

Founded in 1995, Modern Public School has been a beacon of quality education in the region. We believe in nurturing not just academic brilliance but also social responsibility, creativity, and moral values in every student.

Affiliated with CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education), we offer a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to Class XII, supported by world-class infrastructure and a compassionate team of educators.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to become critical thinkers, responsible citizens, and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution fostering excellence, innovation, and compassion in every student we serve.

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your child's future at Modern Public School.

1

Fill Online Form

Complete the registration form with accurate details about the student and family.

2

Document Submission

Submit birth certificate, previous report card, Aadhar card, and passport photos.

3

Interaction / Assessment

Attend a brief interaction session at the school (for Class I and above).

4

Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee online or at the school office to confirm your seat.

5

Welcome to Sunrise!

Collect your welcome kit, uniform details, and join the Modernfamily. 🎉

Age Eligibility

Nursery: 3+ years | LKG: 4+ years | UKG: 5+ years | Class I: 6+ years | Upper classes: As per CBSE norms.
